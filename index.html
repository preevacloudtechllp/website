Frequently Asked Questions

What is your response time for critical incidents? →

We guarantee a 15-minute response time for critical P1 incidents and 1-hour for P2 issues. Our 24/7 SOC team monitors all deployments round-the-clock.

Do you support multi-cloud deployments? →

Yes, we specialize in multi-cloud strategies. Our platform supports AWS, Azure, GCP, and Alibaba Cloud with seamless orchestration and management.

What compliance standards do you support? →

We maintain ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type II, HIPAA, PCI-DSS, and GDPR compliance. We also support industry-specific requirements like FedRAMP and HITRUST.

How long does a typical deployment take? →

Most deployments are completed within 2-4 weeks. Simple migrations can be done in days, while complex multi-infrastructure projects may take 6-8 weeks.

What is your disaster recovery guarantee? →

We provide an RPO (Recovery Point Objective) of 1 hour and RTO (Recovery Time Objective) of 4 hours, backed by our SLA with financial guarantees.
